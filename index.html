Model National Assembly (Model NASS)

Model NASS is a youth legislative simulation created to give young people a direct voice in national decision-making within a fictional but realistic political universe.

Emergency Session: The Boundary Proposal

This emergency session brings together selected youth delegates to deliberate on a national boundary dispute with political, ethnic, and religious implications. Delegates will debate, negotiate, and draft a formal Resolution for action to be transmitted to the National Assembly and the President.

Who Can Apply?

Applications are open to young people interested in governance, policy, law, political simulations, research, and national development. Only 36 delegates will be selected.
