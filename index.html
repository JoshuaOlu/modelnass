Why Model NASS Exists

Following years of youth-led protests and national upheaval, the Federal Republic of Mona established the Model National Assembly — a youth body empowered to deliberate on national crises and submit resolutions directly to the National Assembly and the President.

Model NASS is consultative, urgent, and consequential. Its sessions mirror real legislative pressure, ethical tension, and national responsibility.

The Boundary Crisis

A disputed border between two states, two peoples, and two faiths has erupted again — this time over land rich in coltan, a mineral critical to global technology.

As violence spreads and displacement grows, the Federal Government has summoned the Model National Assembly to deliberate under emergency powers.
